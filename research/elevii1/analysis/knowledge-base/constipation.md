# Constipation on GLP-1s

## Overview
Constipation is a frequent complication when taking GLP-1s like Retatrutide (Reta). Because the medication fundamentally slows down gastric emptying (how fast food processes through the body) and drastically reduces a user's natural urge to eat and drink, the digestive system can easily become backed up. Rather than being a direct side effect of the peptide dose itself, constipation is almost entirely a byproduct of the lifestyle and habit changes induced by the medication.
