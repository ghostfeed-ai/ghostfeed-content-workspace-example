# MOTS-c

## Overview
MOTS-c is a metabolic-targeting peptide often described as an "exercise mimetic" or "cardio in a bottle." It operates at the mitochondrial level to improve how cells produce and use energy. Rather than just suppressing appetite, it shifts the body to burn fuel more efficiently, preventing fuel storage and drastically improving physical endurance, fat metabolism, and cardiovascular output.
