Deemed the "Summer Shred Stack" or the "🔥 Stack," the combination of Retatrutide (Reta), Tesamorelin (Tesa), and MOTS-c is a highly popular and advanced peptide trifecta designed for summer prep and extreme body recomposition. This stack is specifically structured to target fat loss, metabolic output, and recovery simultaneously by attacking the body's fat stores from three entirely different biological pathways.

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **"More is Better" Fallacy:** A massive beginner mistake is aggressively increasing the dosages of these three compounds when the scale stalls. Taking too much Reta completely shuts off appetite (leading to malnourishment) and causes severe nausea. Taking too much Tesa causes headaches, joint tingling, and heavy water retention. Taking too much MOTS-c causes deep physical exhaustion. `[7624614211385101582 | 297900 | 12900 | 272 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7624614211385101582]`, `[7648365271849176333 | 42700 | 1663 | 106 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7648365271849176333]`, `[7645423623670336782 | 35200 | 999 | 104 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7645423623670336782]`
*   **Reta-Specific Side Effects:** 
    *   *Anhedonia:* Reta dampens dopamine/reward pathways. Users may feel emotionally "flat," lose interest in dating, or naturally stop engaging in bad habits like drinking, vaping, or gambling. `[7623169768492633357 | 122600 | 7657 | 393 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7623169768492633357]`, `[7640225000208846094 | 82100 | 6048 | 120 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7640225000208846094]`
    *   *Stimulant & Alcohol Sensitivity:* Reta drastically slows digestion. Alcohol hits slower but much harder, resulting in terrible hangovers. High caffeine/pre-workouts compound with Reta's metabolic boost, causing severe anxiety, high heart rates, and jitters. `[7629404733916187935 | 158200 | 6244 | 211 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7629404733916187935]`, `[7644015680001477902 | 278000 | 17200 | 351 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7644015680001477902]`
*   **Tesa-Specific Problems:**
    *   *Storage & Gelling:* Tesa is highly fragile. Storing it on the refrigerator door handle exposes it to shaking and temperature fluctuations, causing it to degrade. If mixed with cold BAC water or shaken aggressively, it will turn into a useless "jelly." Store on a stable, inner fridge shelf. `[7608743706736200973 | 133500 | 5646 | 104 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7608743706736200973]`, `[7641409821882780941 | 163500 | 7902 | 117 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7641409821882780941]`
    *   *Hunger Spikes:* Tesa increases metabolic recovery, which can sometimes make users feel *more* hungry, battling Reta's suppression. This is normal. `[7648834308022570254 | 4866 | 156 | 16 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7648834308022570254]`
*   **MOTS-c Stalls:** MOTS-c can stall out after a few weeks because it depletes the body's raw materials for energy production. Fixing this requires adding PQQ, CoQ10, and Creatine to refuel the mitochondria. `[7622095890962074894 | 25700 | 756 | 22 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7622095890962074894]`
