Cagrilintide (often abbreviated as "Cag") is a peptide that comes in a raw powder form and must be reconstituted with bacteriostatic water prior to use. Its primary mechanism of action focuses heavily on providing strong fullness signaling to the body. It is often used by individuals looking to maintain steady, consistent appetite control and stay satisfied throughout the entire day.
