# Reconstitution Math & Syringe Conversion

## Overview
Reconstitution is the process of converting stable, raw lyophilized peptide powder into an injectable liquid using bacteriostatic (BAC) water. Because peptides are administered in highly specific micro-doses, understanding the mathematical conversion between milligrams (mg) of powder, milliliters (mL) of BAC water, and "units" on a standard 1mL insulin syringe is the foundational skill required for any protocol. This guide serves as the central hub for all reconstitution math, mixing techniques, injection best practices, and shelf-life logistics across all mentioned compounds.

## Uses / Benefits Mentioned
*   **Precision Dosing:** Mastering the syringe conversions prevents accidental overdosing (which causes severe side effects like nausea and fatigue) or underdosing (which wastes time and money).
*   **Preventing Waste:** Understanding expiration timelines allows users to buy the correct vial sizes so product does not degrade before it can be completely used. 
*   **Side Effect Mitigation:** Proper mixing techniques prevent the compounds from degrading, while precise BAC water ratios can be manipulated (diluted) to prevent injection site stinging. 

## Routines, Timing, Dosage, Or Process Details

### The "Golden Rule" of Reconstitution Math
The simplest way to calculate dosing math is to match the milligrams of the peptide to the milliliters of BAC water. This creates a standard 10-to-1 ratio across the board. 
*   **Formula:** 10mg vial + 1mL (100 units) BAC water = 100 total units.
*   **Conversion:** Under this ratio, 10 units on the syringe equals exactly 1mg. 5 units = 0.5mg. 15 units = 1.5mg. 20 units = 2mg. `[7607251491434286349 | 241600 | 14800 | 301 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7607251491434286349]`, `[7614255427299151118 | 29100 | 1254 | 33 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7614255427299151118]`, `[7642403144353221902 | 37200 | 1141 | 61 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7642403144353221902]`

### The Mixing Process
1.  Wipe the top of the BAC water vial and the peptide vial with an alcohol prep pad.
2.  Extract the desired amount of BAC water using a standard 1mL syringe.
3.  Poke the syringe into the powdered peptide vial at an angle so the water gently trickles down the side of the glass.
4.  Roll and swirl the vial gently in the palms of your hands to dissolve the powder. **Do not shake the vial**, as peptides are fragile. `[7604274823639911711 | 277900 | 14100 | 270 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7604274823639911711]`, `[7641313103233191182 | 40300 | 1255 | 60 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7641313103233191182]`

### Shelf Life & Storage Rules
*   **Raw Powder:** Unmixed, powdered peptides are highly stable and can last 1 to 2 years if kept in a cool, dark place (fridge or freezer preferred), away from heat, direct sunlight, and excessive temperature fluctuations. `[7604270757404773663 | 23400 | 605 | 12 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7604270757404773663]`, `[7645725309572058382 | 57300 | 1366 | 88 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7645725309572058382]`
*   **Mixed Peptides:** Once bacteriostatic water is added, the countdown starts. Mixed vials maintain potency for roughly 90 days. *   **Where to Store:** Vials *must* be stored on an inside, middle shelf of the fridge. Do not store them in the fridge door/handle compartments. ### Injection Sites & Technique
*   Pin in areas with subcutaneous fat for minimal irritation and good blood flow: lower stomach/love handles, upper glutes, outer thigh.
*   **Rotation:** Must rotate injection sites daily/weekly (at least an inch apart) to prevent lipohypertrophy (scar tissue buildup under the skin), which hinders the peptide's ability to absorb into the bloodstream. `[7608301331031330061 | 89400 | 2140 | 50 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7608301331031330061]`, `[7625363226913475854 | 16500 | 501 | 11 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7625363226913475854]`
*   **Prep:** Wait for the alcohol wipe prep to dry completely on the skin before pinning to avoid an initial stinging sensation. Pinch the skin and inject the needle slowly. `[7603879949493112077 | 75600 | 2666 | 83 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7603879949493112077]`

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned

*   **Tesa Turning to "Jelly":** If stored too cold in the fridge, Tesamorelin turns into a jelly-like substance. Fix: Take it out, let it sit at room temperature for 10-15 minutes, and gently swirl it in your hands (never shake). If it remains cloudy/jelly at room temp, it has degraded and lost potency. Prevent this by using 2mL of BAC water instead of 1mL during initial mixing. `[7608743706736200973 | 133500 | 5646 | 104 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7608743706736200973]`, `[7610970723892366606 | 90300 | 3894 | 92 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7610970723892366606]`, `[7641313103233191182 | 40300 | 1255 | 60 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7641313103233191182]`
*   **GHK-CU Injection Stinging & Welting:** Because it is highly concentrated, it commonly causes burning, stinging, or redness/welts at the injection site that can ache all day. 
    *   *Hack 1 (Dilution):* Draw your dose into the syringe, then draw an extra 10-15 units of plain BAC water into the same syringe before injecting to dilute it.
    *   *Hack 2 (Location):* Inject into thicker, less sensitive fat (like the upper glute) rather than the stomach.
    *   *Hack 3 (Temp):* Do not inject it ice-cold. Let the vial sit at room temperature for 30-45 minutes prior to injection. `[7601664372942834974 | 55300 | 1809 | 105 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7601664372942834974]`, `[7623574383633763598 | 24600 | 620 | 40 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7623574383633763598]`, `[7640208992941231373 | 25900 | 783 | 55 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7640208992941231373]`
*   **Blue Skin Dot (GHK-Cu):** Because it is a copper peptide, pinning too shallowly leaves a blue tint on the skin. This is strictly unabsorbed copper, not a bruise, and fades naturally. `[7603573661466987789 | 43100 | 1587 | 49 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7603573661466987789]`
*   **Bad BAC Water:** If BAC water shows discoloration, cloudiness, or any floating particles ("floaters"), throw it away immediately. Using a fresh vial is always cheaper than dealing with injections of spoiled water. `[7632767883646340366 | 167900 | 5149 | 107 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7632767883646340366]`, `[7649187077866704142 | 23500 | 578 | 45 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7649187077866704142]`
*   **Wasting Money on Potency Loss:** Peptides expire 90 days after mixing. Beginners on low doses (e.g., 0.5mg Reta weekly) should only buy 10mg vials. Buying 20mg or 30mg vials at that dose means the compound will expire long before the user can finish it. `[7649080986361253133 | 207800 | 11100 | 439 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7649080986361253133]`
