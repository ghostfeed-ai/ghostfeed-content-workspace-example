# Hair Loss

## Overview
Hair loss is a specific side effect frequently experienced by users on powerful GLP-1 medications and metabolic peptides like Retatrutide (Reta). It typically occurs around the 6 to 12-week mark of a fat-loss protocol. The hair loss is not caused by the medication itself; rather, it is a physical stress response. When the body undergoes rapid weight loss and severe calorie deficits, it shifts its internal resources and focus away from functions like hair growth in order to manage the metabolic stress.
