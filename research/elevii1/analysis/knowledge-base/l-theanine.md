L-Theanine is highlighted in the research notes primarily as a targeted nighttime supplement used to combat side effects caused by advanced peptide protocols. Specifically, it is utilized to counteract the insomnia, restlessness, and sleep disruptions that frequently occur when taking metabolism-boosting or growth-hormone-stimulating peptides like Retatrutide (Reta) and Tesamorelin.
