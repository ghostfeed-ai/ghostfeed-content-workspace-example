# Protein Powder

## Overview
Protein powder is a foundational daily supplement for users on aggressive weight-loss or body-recomposition peptide protocols like Retatrutide (Reta), Tesamorelin, and CJC-1295/Ipamorelin. Because GLP-1s and metabolic peptides drastically suppress appetite and slow down digestion, users often find it physically difficult to consume enough whole foods to meet their daily nutritional requirements. Protein powder is utilized as a highly digestible, efficient tool to hit strict macronutrient goals (typically 0.8 to 1.2 grams of protein per pound of body weight), which is absolutely crucial to preventing muscle loss, metabolic slowdown, and negative cosmetic side effects during rapid fat loss.

## What The Creator Says
The creator heavily emphasizes that peptides are merely tools, and relying on them to just "starve yourself" without prioritizing protein intake is a massive beginner mistake. If a user drops calories severely but neglects their protein macros, the body will burn through muscle tissue alongside fat. This results in a "watery, flat, and weak" physique rather than a lean, toned one. The creator categorizes protein supplementation as a non-negotiable top-tier priority, listing it alongside hydration and sleep as the foundational habits that actually dictate a peptide cycle's long-term success.
