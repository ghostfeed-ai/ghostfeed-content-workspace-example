# Glycine

## Overview
Glycine is a dietary supplement utilized primarily as a nighttime recovery aid. Within the context of peptide protocols, it serves as a targeted solution to combat the sleep disruption, insomnia, and restlessness frequently caused by powerful metabolic peptides. Its primary function in this ecosystem is to actively calm the central nervous system.
