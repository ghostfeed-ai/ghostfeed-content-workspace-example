# Retatrutide + Tirzepatide Stack

## Overview
The Retatrutide (Reta) + Tirzepatide (Tirz) stack is an aggressive, highly targeted protocol designed to double down on maximum appetite suppression. While Retatrutide is a multi-pathway metabolic tool that boosts energy expenditure and fat burning, Tirzepatide acts as a potent, dedicated appetite suppressant. By combining the two, users are able to strictly manage their calorie deficits while maintaining the metabolic advantages of Reta.

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **The "Zero Appetite" Red Flag:** Because this stack doubles down on hunger control, users are at risk of shutting off their appetite completely. If it becomes physically impossible to eat food, the dosage is too high and needs to be lowered. `[7624614211385101582 | 297900 | 12900 | 272 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7624614211385101582]`
*   **Transitioning Dosage Mistakes:** For users switching from Tirzepatide to Retatrutide, a common mistake is matching a high ending dose of Tirz with an equally high starting dose of Reta. This is dangerous and causes severe side effects.
