## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned

### GHK-Cu Issues
*   **"Copper Uglies" (Acne/Breakouts):** A completely normal reaction lasting 1 to 2 weeks. The peptide accelerates skin cell turnover, causing underlying inflammation to surface, making the skin look worse before it clears up. Must remain consistent to push past it. `[7603155182028918047 | 65200 | 3684 | 83 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7603155182028918047]`, `[7616164753806806286 | 79700 | 3141 | 209 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7616164753806806286]`
*   **Stinging & Burning Hacks:** GHK-Cu is highly reactive and stings. Hacks to stop the burn: 1) Never inject it cold straight from the fridge; let the vial sit at room temp for 30-45 minutes. 2) Dilute the dose by drawing an extra 10-15 units of plain BAC water into the syringe before injecting. 3) Pin only in the upper glutes. `[7640208992941231373 | 25900 | 783 | 55 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7640208992941231373]`
*   **Blue Skin Dot:** GHK-Cu liquid is blue. Pinning too shallowly leaves a harmless blue tint on the skin. It is not a bruise and fades naturally as the copper disperses. `[7603573661466987789 | 43100 | 1587 | 49 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7603573661466987789]`
*   **Rosacea / Welts:** Redness and welts lasting 10-15 minutes are common early on. If it triggers lasting rosacea, the dose is too high or taken too frequently. Drop the dose to 1mg to resolve. `[7609415819235249439 | 51800 | 2319 | 58 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7609415819235249439]`
*   **Things to Avoid:** Smoking (constricts blood vessels, slowing collagen), alcohol (weakens skin barrier/dehydrates), and sugar (spikes inflammation and breakouts). `[7613500033866009886 | 209800 | 12600 | 125 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7613500033866009886]`
