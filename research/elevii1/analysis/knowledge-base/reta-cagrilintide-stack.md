This stack combines Reta’s unique ability to act on multiple receptors to boost energy expenditure and burn stored fat, with Cagrilintide’s highly effective mechanism for creating a steady, consistent feeling of physical fullness throughout the day.

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **Muscle Loss from Undereating:** Because Cagrilintide provides such extreme fullness signaling, users often fail to eat enough. It is critical to consume 0.8 to 1 gram of protein per pound of body weight and continue resistance training, otherwise, the weight lost will be muscle, resulting in a flat, weak physique. 
*   **Dehydration & Fatigue:** A lack of hunger often leads to a lack of thirst. Forgetting to drink water causes severe dehydration, headaches, and fatigue.
*   **Rushing the Dose:** Increasing the dosage of either compound before the 2 to 3-week adjustment window is the leading cause of severe side effects, including nausea, acid reflux, diarrhea, and debilitating lethargy.
*   **Digestion Issues:** Reta significantly slows down gastric emptying. Eating heavy, greasy, or highly processed foods on this stack will cause severe bloating, stomach discomfort, and sulfur burps.
