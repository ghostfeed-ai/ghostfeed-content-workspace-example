# Tesamorelin

## Overview
Tesamorelin is a growth-hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) peptide that stimulates the body's natural growth hormone (HGH) pulse. Its primary mechanism of action is increasing IGF-1 levels. It is heavily utilized for targeted body recomposition—specifically burning stubborn visceral fat (fat deep within the organs and midsection)—while preserving muscle mass in a calorie deficit. Beyond fat loss, it is highly valued for improving sleep quality, enhancing physical recovery, and soothing nagging injuries. It is known as a compound that builds up cumulatively over time, requiring strict adherence to fasting protocols to be effective.

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **Puffiness / Water Retention:** Extremely common initial side effect. Increased HGH pulls intracellular water into the muscle tissue. The scale might go up and the body may look softer, but it is not fat gain. Usually lasts 1 to 2 weeks. 
    *   *Debloat Routine:* Low-intensity cardio, drinking 1 gallon of water daily with electrolytes, sitting in a sauna, and eating cucumbers (high water content). `[7609799462852103455 | 78400 | 3574 | 116 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7609799462852103455]`, `[7636609237153615117 | 74600 | 2811 | 124 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7636609237153615117]`
*   **"Jelly" Tesamorelin (Gelling Issue):** If mixed improperly or stored too cold, Tesa turns into a cloudy jelly. 
    *   *Prevention Hacks:* 1) Use 2mL of BAC water instead of 1mL to give it more dissolving room. 2) Let cold BAC water reach room temperature before mixing. 3) Gently swirl the vial in your hands—DO NOT SHAKE. 
    *   *Crisis Fix:* If it turns to jelly in the fridge, take it out, let it sit at room temp for 10-15 minutes, and gently swirl it until clear. `[7608743706736200973 | 133500 | 5646 | 104 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7608743706736200973]`, `[7641313103233191182 | 40300 | 1255 | 60 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7641313103233191182]`
*   **Red Flags (Dose is Too High):** Constant water retention beyond the adjustment phase, frequent headaches (especially after a dose increase), numbness/tingling in the hands, joint pain, and feeling run down. `[7625746620616920334 | 46500 | 1282 | 93 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7625746620616920334]`, `[7648365271849176333 | 42700 | 1663 | 106 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7648365271849176333]`
*   **Insomnia / Sleep Issues:** Because Tesa increases energy via GH signaling, taking it at night can make winding down difficult for some users. *Fix:* Switch to morning doses, lower evening screen time/stimuli, and add L-Theanine + Magnesium Bisglycinate. `[7636232429505727758 | 28900 | 838 | 66 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7636232429505727758]`
