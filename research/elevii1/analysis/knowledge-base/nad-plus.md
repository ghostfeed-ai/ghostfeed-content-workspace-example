## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **"Too Much NAD" Red Flags:** Taking too high of a dose leads to three consistent negative side effects:
    1.  Consistent, lingering headaches.
    2.  Sleeping issues (trouble falling asleep or staying asleep).
    3.  Brain fog (feeling scattered, forgetful, or unable to think clearly). `[7646963001618189581 | 42900 | 995 | 91 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7646963001618189581]`
