# Ipamorelin

## Overview
In the provided research notes, Ipamorelin is almost exclusively discussed in tandem with CJC-1295 (specifically CJC-1295 No DAC). Together, they form a highly popular peptide blend used as a growth hormone secretagogue. This blend tells the pituitary gland to secrete its own natural form of growth hormone multiple times a day. While CJC-1295 increases growth hormone over time, Ipamorelin causes a clean, immediate growth hormone release without impacting stress hormones. 

## What The Creator Says
The creator considers the CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin blend to be an "absolute no-brainer" for full-body recomposition over a 3 to 6-month period. He frequently positions it as a "10/10 duo" when paired with weight loss peptides, and ranks it as one of the top 3 peptides for muscle growth. He notes that there are "no two better peps to be pairing together" than CJC/Ipamorelin and Tesamorelin for ultimate body recomposition.

## Uses / Benefits Mentioned
*   **Natural Growth Hormone Production:** Signals the pituitary gland to release natural human growth hormone (HGH) continuously throughout the day and night.
*   **Muscle Preservation & Growth:** Promotes lean muscle mass, enhances protein synthesis, and helps the body maintain a fuller, more athletic, and muscular appearance, especially when in a calorie deficit.
*   **Sleep & Recovery:** Heavily supports deep sleep, tissue repair, and overall physical recovery from workouts. 
*   **Long-Term Body Recomposition:** Provides consistent GH signaling that yields gradual, long-term physique changes rather than just rapid fat loss.
