# Tesamorelin Fasting Protocol

## Overview
Tesamorelin is a growth hormone-releasing peptide (GHRH) designed to stimulate the body's natural growth hormone (GH) pulse. While highly effective for burning visceral fat and improving body composition, its success relies entirely on a strict fasting protocol. If Tesamorelin is taken while the body is processing food, the resulting insulin spike will directly blunt and block the growth hormone signal, rendering the peptide ineffective. To prevent this, users must adhere to a strict 2 to 3-hour fasting window around the time of their injection.

## What The Creator Says
The creator heavily emphasizes that taking Tesamorelin is a waste of time and money if the fasting rules are ignored. He explains that eating food—specifically carbohydrates and fats—spikes the body's insulin levels. Because insulin and growth hormone cannot peak at the same time, any insulin in the bloodstream will immediately shut down the GH pulse that Tesamorelin is trying to create. He insists that establishing a rock-solid, consistent daily fasting window (either in the morning or at night) is the single most important factor for success on this compound.
