# Insomnia & Sleep Disruption

## Overview
Insomnia, restlessness, and sleep disruptions are frequent side effects when taking metabolic-boosting peptides like Retatrutide (Reta), cellular energy boosters like NAD and MOTS-c, or growth hormone-releasing peptides like Tesamorelin (Tesa). Because these compounds increase resting energy expenditure, amplify caffeine sensitivity, or trigger internal signaling, they can make it difficult for the body to wind down. Conversely, sleep is the critical window when the body naturally releases growth hormone and repairs tissue, making it a vital pillar for peptide efficacy. When dosed and timed correctly, certain peptides (like CJC-1295/Ipamorelin) and specific supplement stacks are utilized to actively combat these disruptions and induce deep, restorative sleep.
