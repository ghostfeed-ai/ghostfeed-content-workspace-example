# Retatrutide Cardio & Workout Routine

## Overview
When taking Retatrutide (Reta), your diet and workout routines are strictly required for success; the peptide is a tool, not a magic shortcut. Because Reta is a powerful metabolic tool that drastically reduces calorie intake and actively pushes your resting energy expenditure higher, the body requires a very specific training stimulus to prevent muscle loss, central nervous system (CNS) burnout, and progress stalls. The overarching protocol mandates consistent resistance training to hold onto muscle, paired with Low-Intensity Steady State (LISS) cardio, while strictly avoiding high-intensity interval training (HIIT) or heavy endurance cardio.
