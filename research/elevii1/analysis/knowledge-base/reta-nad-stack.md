# Retatrutide + NAD+ Stack

## Overview
The Retatrutide (Reta) + NAD+ stack is designed to create a balance between aggressive fat loss and high daily energy. Retatrutide acts as the foundational metabolic tool, heavily reducing food noise and suppressing appetite to enforce a calorie deficit. However, this lack of food intake inherently drains the body's energy. NAD+ is introduced to counteract this depletion, stepping in to directly boost cellular energy, increase ATP production, and support overall cellular recovery so the user doesn't feel sluggish or depleted while losing weight.

    1.  *Consistent headaches.*
    2.  *Sleeping issues* (trouble falling asleep or staying asleep).
    3.  *Paradoxical Brain Fog* (feeling scattered, forgetful, and unable to think clearly, completely defeating the purpose of the peptide). [7646963001618189581 | 42900 | 995 | 91 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7646963001618189581]
