# Retatrutide + MOTS-c Stack

## Overview
The combination of Retatrutide (Reta) and MOTS-c is highly regarded as a synergistic pairing that addresses fat loss and energy from two entirely different biological pathways. Often referred to as "cardio in a bottle," this stack combines Reta’s ability to drastically reduce "food noise" and enforce a calorie deficit (controlling the "input") with MOTS-c’s ability to optimize mitochondrial function and physical endurance (improving the "output"). The primary goal of this stack is to prevent the severe fatigue and sluggishness that typically accompanies eating fewer calories on Reta, allowing the user to maintain top-tier athletic performance and burn fat efficiently.

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **MOTS-c Overdose / Stalls:** Taking too much MOTS-c does not increase endurance; it leaves the user feeling completely drained, exhausted, experiencing frequent headaches, and feeling "flat" with weird energy in the gym. `[7645423623670336782 | 35200 | 999 | 104 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7645423623670336782]`. *   **The "No Cardio" Mistake:** Skipping cardio on this stack is a massive mistake. MOTS-c requires daily consistent cardio (walking, incline, biking) to drive metabolism and calorie burn. Skipping it leads to sluggishness and blunts progress. However, avoid High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) or long runs, as Reta already stresses the CNS, and extreme cardio causes severe fatigue and high cortisol. `[7611340265818819870 | 118200 | 8875 | 168 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7611340265818819870]`, `[7634289107195251982 | 69300 | 2503 | 104 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7634289107195251982]`
*   **Dietary Mismanagement (Muscle Loss):** Relying solely on Reta to starve yourself causes muscle loss and a slowed metabolism. You must prioritize protein (0.8g - 1g per lb of body weight) and lift weights 3-4 times a week, otherwise the body has no reason to hold onto muscle. `[7619434176307088654 | 70400 | 4646 | 64 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7619434176307088654]`, `[7643926538244640013 | 23200 | 596 | 12 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7643926538244640013]`
*   **Reta + Stimulant Anxiety:** Reta naturally elevates the resting heart rate and metabolism. Stacking it with high-caffeine energy drinks, heavy pre-workouts, or nicotine causes severe anxiety, jitters, and extended insomnia because Reta slows down digestion, prolonging the stimulant effect. `[7624303033526013198 | 19300 | 628 | 33 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7624303033526013198]`, `[7643621541871144206 | 52600 | 1470 | 46 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7643621541871144206]`
