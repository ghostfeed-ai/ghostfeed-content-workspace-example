# Klow Blend

## Overview
Klow is a specialized, multi-compound peptide blend consisting of four distinct peptides: **KPV, GHK-Cu, BPC-157, and TB-500**. While many peptide blends focus purely on cosmetics or joint repair, the unique inclusion of KPV allows the Klow blend to simultaneously address internal digestion, physical tissue recovery, and cosmetic anti-aging benefits.

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **Impatience:** A major mistake users make is increasing their dosage quickly out of frustration if immediate visual changes don't appear. More peptide does not equal better results. Users must stop obsessing over day-to-day changes and look at weekly trends `[7642420841061354766 | 245300 | 13000 | 287 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7642420841061354766]`.
*   **GHK-Cu Stinging:** Because Klow contains GHK-Cu, users may experience stinging or burning upon injection. This can be mitigated by injecting into less sensitive areas with thicker fat (like the glutes instead of the stomach), letting the vial sit at room temperature for 30-45 minutes before injecting, and diluting the dose with an extra 10-15 units of plain bacteriostatic water in the syringe `[7623574383633763598 | 24600 | 620 | 40 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7623574383633763598]`, `[7640208992941231373 | 25900 | 783 | 55 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7640208992941231373]`.
