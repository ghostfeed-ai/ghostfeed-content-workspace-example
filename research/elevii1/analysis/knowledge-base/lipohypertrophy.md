# Lipohypertrophy

## Overview
Lipohypertrophy is a condition characterized by a buildup of scar tissue under the skin. In the context of peptide and GLP-1 usage, it is a physical side effect caused directly by injecting medication into the exact same location repeatedly. This localized tissue buildup acts as a barrier, preventing the injected peptides from absorbing properly into the bloodstream and ultimately rendering the medication ineffective.
