# Copper Uglies (GHK-Cu Breakouts)

## Overview
"Copper Uglies" is a colloquial term for a common, temporary phase of acne and skin inflammation that occurs during the first few weeks of starting a GHK-Cu (copper peptide) protocol. Because GHK-Cu is highly reactive and drastically accelerates skin cell turnover and tissue regeneration, it forces underlying, dormant inflammation to surface all at once. This causes the skin to visibly look worse (breakouts, irritation, redness) before it gets better. It is a completely normal biological reaction that typically resolves in 1 to 3 weeks as the body adapts and the skin barrier strengthens. 

## What The Creator Says
The creator heavily emphasizes that the Copper Uglies are an expected "adjustment phase" and not a sign that the peptide is failing. He notes that accelerated skin repair naturally pushes impurities to the surface, and users must remain consistent to push past this phase. He specifically warns beginners against panicking and either quitting the protocol or increasing their dosage out of frustration. He stresses that "more does not equal better" and that consistency, rather than a higher dose, is what eventually clears the complexion. 

## Uses / Benefits Mentioned
While the Copper Uglies are an initial hurdle, pushing past them yields the primary cosmetic benefits of GHK-Cu:
*   **Skin Health:** Dramatically improves skin elasticity, texture, and brightness. Clears up acne and acne scarring long-term once the initial breakout phase passes. 
*   **Anti-Aging & Collagen:** Signals the body to naturally produce collagen and repair tissues, which is highly beneficial for preventing loose skin during rapid fat loss. 
*   **Hair & Nails:** Promotes hair regrowth (fuller, thicker, less shedding) and makes nails grow faster and stronger (less brittle). 
*   *Note:* Experiencing even 1-2 of these benefits (e.g., skin glow and thicker hair) is proof the peptide is working.
