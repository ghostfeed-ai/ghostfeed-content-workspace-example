## Overview

Originally designed for diabetics, this product has been adapted within the peptide community as a travel hack for flying and traveling without ruining the potency of reconstituted compounds.

## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
While the case itself does not have side effects, it solves a major logistical problem for peptide users: **Peptide Degradation.** 
Once peptides (such as Tesamorelin, Retatrutide, MOTS-c, or GHK-Cu) are mixed with bacteriostatic water, a 90-day countdown begins, and they *must* remain refrigerated. Exposure to constant temperature fluctuations or heat destroys the peptides (e.g., turning Tesamorelin into "jelly").
