# Retatrutide Diet Protocol

## Overview
The Retatrutide (Reta) diet protocol centers on the biological reality that Reta drastically slows down gastric emptying while simultaneously increasing the body's resting metabolic rate. Because appetite is severely suppressed, every meal must count. The creator stresses that Reta is a tool, not a shortcut to starve yourself. Relying entirely on the peptide without optimizing nutrition leads to severe muscle loss, slowed metabolism, and an undesirable "flat, watery" physique. The protocol requires a high protein intake, easily digestible simple carbohydrates, strict moderation of dietary fats, and eating smaller, frequent meals rather than large, heavy sittings.
