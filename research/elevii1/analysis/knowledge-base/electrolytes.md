# Electrolytes

## Overview
Electrolytes are positioned as a mandatory foundational supplement for anyone running peptide protocols, specifically GLP-1 medications like Retatrutide (Reta). Because these compounds heavily suppress appetite and "food noise," users frequently experience a drastic, unintentional drop in their daily food and water intake. Supplementing with electrolytes is essential to combat the severe dehydration, fatigue, and digestive side effects that arise from this reduced consumption.
