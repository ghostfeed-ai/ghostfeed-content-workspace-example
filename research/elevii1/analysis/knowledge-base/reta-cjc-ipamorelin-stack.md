## Side Effects, Problems, Or Mistakes Mentioned
*   **The "More is Better" Fallacy:** The #1 beginner mistake is titrating up the Reta dose too fast out of impatience. *   **Stimulant Stacking:** Reta naturally elevates resting heart rate. Stacking it with high-caffeine pre-workouts, energy drinks, or nicotine compounds central nervous system stimulation, causing severe anxiety, jitters, and worsened dehydration. `[7643621541871144206 | 52600 | 1470 | 46 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7643621541871144206]`
*   **Digestion & Alcohol:** Reta slows gastric emptying. Alcohol is processed much slower, leading to a delayed but heavily amplified intoxicating effect, followed by brutal hangovers and nausea. *   **Constipation:** Almost exclusively caused by dehydration and severe calorie restriction.
