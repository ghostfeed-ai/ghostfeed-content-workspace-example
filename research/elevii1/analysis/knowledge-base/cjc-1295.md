# CJC-1295

## Overview
CJC-1295 is a growth hormone secretagogue that stimulates the pituitary gland to secrete its natural form of growth hormone. It is primarily categorized into two different versions: **CJC with DAC** and **CJC No DAC**. 
*   **CJC with DAC:** Long-acting, keeps human growth hormone (HGH) elevated all week, and is taken once a week. This is best for convenience. 
*   **CJC No DAC:** Short-acting, spikes HGH quickly but only for a few hours, and must be taken daily to align with the body's natural pulses. 

CJC-1295 (No DAC) is almost universally paired and blended with **Ipamorelin**. While CJC increases the overall baseline of HGH over time, Ipamorelin causes a clean growth hormone release without impacting stress hormones. Together, they provide continuous, consistent natural growth hormone signaling day and night.
