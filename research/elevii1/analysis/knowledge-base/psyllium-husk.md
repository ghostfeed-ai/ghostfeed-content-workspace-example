*   **Overdoing Fiber Supplements:** While psyllium husk is great for binding loose stools, a major mistake users make is taking *too much* fiber while on Reta. Because Reta drastically slows down digestion and gastric emptying, excessive fiber can sit in the gut and cause severe bloating, indigestion, stomach pain, and nausea.
