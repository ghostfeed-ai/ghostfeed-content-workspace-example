# Retatrutide + Tesamorelin Stack

## Overview
Deemed the "Skinny Fat Stack" or the "Best Fat Loss Stack," combining Retatrutide (Reta) and Tesamorelin (Tesa) creates a highly synergistic protocol that attacks fat loss from two completely different biological pathways. Retatrutide acts as the foundational input controller—it suppresses appetite, eliminates "food noise," improves insulin sensitivity, and increases metabolic energy expenditure. Tesamorelin acts as the output optimizer—a growth hormone-releasing peptide that specifically targets and burns stubborn visceral fat (deep belly fat) while promoting muscle preservation, recovery, and deep sleep. Together, they create simultaneous metabolic boosting and drastic body recomposition, allowing users to lean out the midsection rapidly without losing muscle mass.
