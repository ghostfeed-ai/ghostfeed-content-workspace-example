## 3. Background & Location Patterns
The settings are exclusively domestic, indoor environments that feel modern, clean, but distinctly casual.
*   **Locations:** Filmed in modern home interiors, including bedrooms, living rooms, hallways, residential kitchens, or hotel rooms. 
*   **Background Elements:** The backgrounds lack typical "influencer" neon lights or gym aesthetics. They feature plain white/neutral walls, closed light-wooden doors, closet doors, unmade beds in the background, or modern dark kitchen cabinetry. 
*   **Distinctive Decor:** One recurring background features modern light wood paneling/slats and a vertical display of three blue skateboards mounted on the wall. `[7637997088277548301 | 37600 | 1405 | 75 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7637997088277548301]`
*   **Purpose:** By never filming the talking-head segments in a gym or a clinic, the creator establishes a "guy next door giving advice" vibe, making extreme physique and medical protocols feel accessible to the average viewer.

## 4. On-Screen Text (OST) Strategy
The creator uses a hyper-standardized, persistent on-screen text structure that acts as the visual anchor for every single talking-head video.
*   **Placement & Persistence:** A high-contrast text block (usually white text on a black background, or vice-versa) is placed dead-center on the screen, resting over the creator's chest/torso area. It appears in the first frame of the video and remains on-screen for the entire duration. Dynamic auto-captions run simultaneously at the bottom of the screen.
*   **The "Parenthetical Formula":** The text is almost universally formatted as a main title followed by a parenthetical clarifier, hook, or call to action. 
    *   *Examples:* `GHK-CU for dummies (dosing + mixing)`, `Reta + Anhedonia (why you feel numb)`, `Reta Face (how to avoid it)`. `[All source videos]`
*   **Utility:** This text ensures that even if a user is scrolling quickly without sound, they instantly know the exact hyper-niche medical topic being discussed without waiting through an intro.

## 8. How Visuals Support Peptide Education
The overarching visual strategy is designed to **demystify and de-medicalize** peptides.
*   By dressing like a standard fitness enthusiast and filming in a bedroom or hallway, the creator removes the intimidating barrier of sterile clinic environments. 
*   The use of strict text boxes categorizes complex chemical names (Tesamorelin, Retatrutide, MOTS-c) into easily digestible, highly searchable "dummy guides."
*   By relying on gestures instead of actual needles, the content remains safe for platforms like TikTok while focusing the viewer's attention purely on the math, the science, and the lifestyle integration.
