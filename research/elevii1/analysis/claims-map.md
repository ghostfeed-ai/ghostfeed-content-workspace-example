## 3. Reconstitution, Storage & Logistics Claims

*   **The "For Dummies" Reconstitution Rule:** Always match milligrams to milliliters of bacteriostatic water. For a 10mg vial, add 1ml of BAC water (which is 100 syringe units). This makes 1mg = 10 units, universally simplifying the math. `[7614255427299151118 | 29100 | 1254 | 33 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7614255427299151118]`, `[7646857326707739917 | 16600 | 517 | 42 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7646857326707739917]`
*   **Bacteriostatic (BAC) Water Shelf Life:** Unopened, it lasts until the printed expiration date. Once the seal is punctured, the countdown starts: it expires and degrades after 28-30 days. Discard immediately if cloudy or showing floating particles. `[7632767883646340366 | 167900 | 5149 | 107 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7632767883646340366]`, `[7649187077866704142 | 23500 | 578 | 45 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7649187077866704142]`
*   **Mixed Peptide Shelf Life:** Once powder is mixed with BAC water, potency degrades after 90 days. *Hack:* Beginners on low Reta doses should buy 10mg vials, not 30mg vials, to avoid the product expiring before they can use it. `[7604270757404773663 | 23400 | 605 | 12 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7604270757404773663]`, `[7649080986361253133 | 207800 | 11100 | 439 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7649080986361253133]`
*   **Raw Powder Shelf Life:** In raw dry powder form, peptides are highly stable and last 1 to 2 years if kept away from heat, light, and massive temperature fluctuations (fridge/freezer). `[7645725309572058382 | 57300 | 1366 | 88 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7645725309572058382]`
*   **Storage Mistake:** Never store mixed vials on the refrigerator door handle compartments. *   **Injection Sites:** Rotate injection sites weekly (stomach, thigh, glute) to prevent lipohypertrophy (lumps of scar tissue under the skin), which stops the peptide from absorbing into the bloodstream. `[7625363226913475854 | 16500 | 501 | 11 | https://www.tiktok.com/@elevii1/video/7625363226913475854]`
